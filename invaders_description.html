Description:

Space Invaders is a fixed shooter in which the player moves a laser cannon horizontally across the bottom of the screen and fires at aliens overhead

The goal is to eliminate all the aliens by shooting them. Aliens will attempt to destroy the player's ship by firing projectiles. The game ends when your ship is destroyed


Controls:

The user controls the direction of the spaceship

(left or right) using the keys W and D, and spacebar to shoot.
